L'éatarde Art

Art is a three letter word with a thousand letters full of meaning.
From a very young age, I have always been creative: I expressed my creativity in all sorts of ways, and just like all of us, ended up creating all sorts of works of art, in all different forms, styles and meaning. It was only until I enrolled in Law school, and endured the endless yet somewhat interesting lectures, that I started to express my creativity with that what I had in hand, a pencil, on that what I had in front of me: a piece of paper. When I graduated from Law school, I was a trained drawer, and my passion from drawing has only but intensified ever since.

Techniques

Pencil

My favourite technique is pencil. I love taking my time and gradually bringing the beloved object to life. I am always fascinated by how the once empty piece of paper turns into a story into which so much emotion and love has been poured into.
I like to draw the donkeys and goats of Weinfeldermaar, but I also like drawing flowers or people I encounter during my walks.
