The Seven Donkeys of Weinfeldermaar

When you walk around Weinfeldermaar, you will spot seven donkeys grazing the area. Their names are:
Grandma, Uncle George, Aunt Lila and her daughter Lynn, Mum and her daughters Donkey Sonia and Donkey Victoria.
